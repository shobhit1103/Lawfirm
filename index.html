A S PARTNERS, SOLICITORS & ADVOCATES is a Law Firm which advices the India’s leading companies and institutions. We are known for our prowess in helping our clients resolve their most demanding business challenges and provide practical and innovative advice to our clients and tackle the root and not just the symptoms of a problem. Through years of experience representing different organizations and institutions all over National Capital Region, our lawyers provide a unique understanding of all legal issues. Our approach is responsive, innovative and practical. The core philosophy of the firm remains client satisfaction. The firm places a premium on transparency and high quality legal advice at competitive costs. We consider the practice of law to be, first and foremost, about our clients. We are resolute in our commitment to exceptional client service. We are recognized for the breadth and depth of our legal expertise and experience and we apply that expertise and experience to help our clients succeed. We do this by understanding our clients’ objectives, by developing a strong grasp of their business needs and their industry requirements, and by building trusted relationships with their people. A S PARTNERS has well balanced representation of individual and corporate clientele and represents a cross section of industry including State Bank of India, Delhi Development Authority (DDA), RMB Financial Services, CL Products (India) Limited and Shri Lal Mahal Group of Companies etc.
